Creamy Baked Mac-N-Cheese Recipe

Description

Most people aren't used to cooking mac-n-cheese.

If you're hesitant to try making homemade mac and cheese, don't worry! The recipe breaks it down into simple steps that are easy to follow Plus, the ingredients are affordable and require no special kitchen equipment So why not give it a try and enjoy a delicious, comforting meal that is sure to become a family favorite?

I highly recommend trying the Creamy Baked Mac and Cheese recipe from The Chunky Chef.

Ingredients

  • 1lb. dried elbow pasta
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups whole milk
  • 2 1/2 cups half and half
  • 4 cups shredded medium sharp cheddar cheese
  • 2 cups shredded Gruyere cheese
  • 1/2Tbsp. salt
  • 1/2 tsp. black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp. paprika
